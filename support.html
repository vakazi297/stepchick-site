Frequently Asked Questions

Why aren't my steps showing up?

Make sure you've granted StepChick access to Apple Health. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → StepChick and enable Steps. It may take a moment for data to sync.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find StepChick Glow+ and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes! Open StepChick on your new device and tap "Restore Purchases" on the subscription screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How are Glow Points calculated?

Glow Points are earned based on your daily step count synced from Apple Health. The more you walk, the more you glow!

Is my health data shared with anyone?

No. StepChick reads step data from Apple Health locally on your device. Your health information is never uploaded to external servers or shared with third parties.
